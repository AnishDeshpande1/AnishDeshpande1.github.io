Janamdin to keval zindagi ka aks dikhata hai,
Saara waqt ghiste rahe,
Aur waqt yu hi nikal jaata hai;

Ehthiyaat baratkar tumne har kashti ko chalaya hai,
Dilchaspi to tumhe falak mei apna chhap chhodne ki thi,
Lekin ISRO ne ek chhatke mei uss kashti ko dubaya hai;

Jinke saath waqt beetana apne laazmi na samjha,
Thoda waqt unke liye bhi nikal lo;
Arre kitna kaam karoge yaar,
Thoda maza apni jholi mei bhi daal lo;

Arre itna kyu bhaagti hai Satlab aur LCH ke beech mei,
Thodi der khaane par logon se guftagu kar,
Cycling nhi seekhi usko shuru kar;

Mei bas itna kehna chata hu,
Itna bhaag mat, thoda tham ja,
Ab aur kuchh to aata nahi tha mujhe,
To maine chand alfaaz likhna hi munasif samjha
